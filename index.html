Work

I took this position initially as a winter internship during school break. However as the time for it to end came near they offered to have it extend until I graduated in 2022. This position was primarily to introduce me to an actual work environment as well as the technologies, techniques, and time management skill to work both in the office and as a remote worker. I worked by myself with daily meetings with my boss to go over what progress I had made and my thoughts on what I would work on that day and how I would approach it. I also had both weekly and biweekly meetings with other teams to schedule upcoming projects that we believed could be done in 2 weeks as well as report how we were doing on our projects which were managed using a website called Asana. Most of my projects were creating forms for mainly faculty to submit to request funding from the school or to fix bugs that had appeared on other forms and were reported to our department. Storing the information collected in the forms was primarily done through SQL while the forms themselves were created using CFML, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. A significant project that I did in this position would be the complete reconstruction of the Hamilton College Dining Menu section of their college application available through the apple and android app store. Using AJAX I did a POST call to Bon Appetit's API that returned an enourmos JSON object that I verified and stored using SQL. I then would parse that data and display menus for multiple dining halls each with multiple meal times in both mobile and desktop friendly layouts as well as providing a way for people to look at prior and future menus.

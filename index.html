  Cybersecurity affects everybody including the government. It is not something that being big is a positive thing but is instead a problem as there are more possible vulnerabilities. There is more code that could be possibly exploited or more people to take advantage of. There is also the higher possibility of more important data being stolen as a result of bigger companies like Amazon collecting more data including but not limited to credit card information or previous purchases. The formal definition by CISCO of cybersecurity is "Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and program from digital attacks."
  The CIA Triad stands for confidentiality, integrity, and availability and is the origin of nearly all security policies. Confidentiality refers to protecting private information from unauthorized eyes. Some ways to protect confidentiality is having different levels of access to different levels of data. For example, you might not want to give access to vital data to someone who is in a temporary role or was just hired. You could also encrypt the data so that it is not so easily accessed and read and/or you could require that someone identifies themself before having access to the data. Integrity is the idea that the data cannot be edited, deleted, or added without the consent of the relevant party. One way to prevent this is to secure who can access and change the data. Another way is to have backups of the data such that if someone does get in and modify the data then it can be restored. Availability is the idea that the data should be available to the person to whom it belongs such as bank statements to the owners of the account. This increases the security because the data owner has the ability to manually confirm and notice any changes that they did not expect. For the company this means that there servers and networks need to be monitored and up so that they can be accessed.

  Cybersecurity is constantly being expanded as the internet is introduced into different fields and objects. Cybersecurity encompasses many skills.
  Security Engineering is a category that includes Information Security (InfoSec) protects data in any form from being accessed, modified, shared, or deleted by the wrong people. Network Security regards the network infrastructure of an organization that guards against unauthorized access or data being intercepted. Application Security refers to techniques used to defend an application (both hardware and software) from attack they include but are not limited to secure coding, using antivirus, firewalls, and encryption. Cloud Security is making sure resources uploaded to the cloud are secure. Cryptography methods to hide and unhide information so that data is readable only by authorized people. Critical Infrastructure Security is defending physical systems that are beecoming more digital like energy grids, hospitals, water, waste, schools, etc. This category includes not only human attacks but natural disasters.
  Governance And Compliance it is extremely important to be informed and to follow the laws and regulations put in place by the state, nation, and even international agencies. You must also be sure to audit your security to ensure that those policies are followed. This part of cybersecurity does not necessarily require programming information.
  Risk Management And Threat Intelligence there will always be a risk of someone getting past your security measures. You can manage that risk by identifying the risks, assessing the chance and severity of the risks, and finding the best ways to most efficiently address those risks. You should also attempt to identify the motivations behind the attacks, how severe the attacks might be, and the possible ways in which they might attack. Because of all this information it often overlaps with the data science field.
  Security Operations is the area responsible for creating security principles, monitoring incidents, and recovering from disasters. They can be seen as the people that actually do the above mentioned parts as well as creating documents and plans to explain the policy to other people.
  Education this idea is that technology and security is only as strong as the people who use them. It teaches people how to follow the security policy of the company and maintain their own personal cybersecurity.
